Dr. Wittenberns received her BA in Biology and Marine Science from Boston University. She then received her DVM from University of Florida in 2015 (Go Gators!) and completed her Small Animal Rotating Internship at Carolina Veterinary Specialists in Charlotte, NC. She went on to complete a Medical Oncology Internship and Residency at Colorado State University in 2020. While in Ft. Collins, she also received a Masters degree in Veterinary Clinical Sciences. Dr. Wittenberns has a special interest in personalized multi-modal cancer therapy for pets and their families. She also has an interest in palliative care and feline cancers. Dr. Wittenberns is excited to trade in the snow for the sun and for all that Austin has to offer. She enjoys exploring outdoors, spinning, and spending time with her Ragdoll cat, Beau.
